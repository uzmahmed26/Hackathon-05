More Questions?

Everything you need to know about TechCorp AI Support.

Our AI agent responds within seconds for most requests. High-priority tickets receive a reply in under 2 minutes, medium in under 5 minutes, and low-priority within 30 minutes — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

The AI handles ~81% of all tickets automatically — including product questions, how-to guides, troubleshooting, bug report intake, billing inquiries, and feedback. Complex issues like refund disputes, pricing negotiations, and legal matters are escalated to a human agent.

When the AI detects that a ticket requires human attention — based on sentiment, complexity, or category — it automatically routes it to the right human agent with full context. You'll be notified via email and can expect contact within 30 minutes during business hours.

Yes! TechCorp supports 3 channels: this web form, Email at support@techcorp.io, and WhatsApp at +1 (800) TECHCORP. All channels are monitored by the same AI agent, and your conversation history is unified across them.

After submitting a ticket you'll receive a Ticket ID (e.g. TK-001). Use the tab above to enter your ID and see the full conversation history and current status in real time.

Absolutely. All communications are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. We use JWT-based authentication, role-based access control, and our data infrastructure runs on enterprise-grade cloud with regular security audits. We never share your data with third parties.

The AI continuously monitors its own confidence score. If it cannot resolve an issue with high confidence, it flags the ticket for escalation and transfers it to a specialist — along with a full summary of the conversation so you never have to repeat yourself.
