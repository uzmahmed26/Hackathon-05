# TechCorp Company Profile

## Overview
TechCorp is a leading provider of cloud-based project management solutions designed specifically for small to medium-sized businesses. Our mission is to simplify project coordination, enhance team collaboration, and drive productivity through intuitive and powerful tools.

## Product: ProjectFlow Pro
ProjectFlow Pro is our flagship project management software that helps teams plan, execute, and monitor their projects efficiently. Our platform combines ease of use with robust functionality to meet the diverse needs of modern businesses.

### Key Features
- **Task Management**: Create, assign, and track tasks with due dates, priorities, and dependencies
- **Team Collaboration**: Real-time messaging, file sharing, and comment threads on tasks
- **Time Tracking**: Built-in timer and timesheet functionality for accurate project billing
- **Resource Allocation**: Visualize team workload and balance assignments effectively
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive dashboards and analytics for project insights
- **Calendar Integration**: Sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar
- **Gantt Charts**: Visual timeline view for complex project planning
- **Custom Workflows**: Tailor the system to match your team's unique processes

## Target Customers
Our primary market consists of small to medium businesses across various industries including:
- Technology startups
- Marketing agencies
- Consulting firms
- Construction companies
- Creative studios
- Healthcare organizations
- Educational institutions

We serve businesses with 5-200 employees who need structure and visibility in their project management but lack the resources for enterprise-level solutions.
