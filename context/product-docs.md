# TechCorp ProjectFlow Pro - Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### 1. How do I create a new account?
To create a new account, visit our website and click on "Sign Up". Enter your email address, choose a password, and complete the registration form. You'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

### 2. Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all new users. During this period, you'll have access to all Professional Plan features with no commitment required.

### 3. How do I invite team members to my project?
Navigate to your project dashboard, click on "Settings", then "Team Members". Enter the email addresses of the people you want to invite and select their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer).

### 4. Can I import data from other project management tools?
Yes, we support importing data from popular tools like Trello, Asana, and Monday.com. Go to "Settings" > "Import" to access the import wizard.

### 5. How do I set up my first project?
Click on "New Project" from your dashboard, give your project a name, select a template if desired, and customize the workflow. Then add your first tasks and assign team members.

## Features

### 6. How do I create and assign tasks?
Click the "+" button in your project view to create a new task. Enter the task name, description, due date, and assign it to a team member using the assignee dropdown menu.

### 7. Can I track time spent on tasks?
Yes, each task has a built-in timer. Click the clock icon next to a task to start the timer. You can also manually enter time spent in the time tracking section.

### 8. What reporting options are available?
Our platform offers various reports including project progress, team workload, time tracking, and custom reports. Access these from the "Reports" section in your dashboard.

### 9. How do I use Gantt charts?
Switch to the Gantt view from your project dashboard. Drag and drop tasks to adjust timelines, create dependencies, and visualize your project schedule.

### 10. Can I customize workflows?
Yes, in the Business Plan, you can create custom workflows tailored to your team's processes. Go to "Settings" > "Workflows" to configure your custom statuses and transitions.

### 11. How do file attachments work?
Click on any task to open the task details panel. Use the paperclip icon to upload files directly to the task. All team members with access can view and download these files.

### 12. What integration options are available?
We integrate with popular tools like Slack, Google Drive, Dropbox, Salesforce, and many others. Visit "Settings" > "Integrations" to connect your preferred tools.

### 13. Can I set up recurring tasks?
Yes, when creating or editing a task, you can set it to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Find this option in the task scheduling section.

## Pricing

### 14. What's included in the Starter Plan?
The Starter Plan includes up to 5 projects, basic task management, standard reporting, and email support. It's ideal for individuals or very small teams.

### 15. How does billing work?
Billing is monthly and per-user. You'll be charged on the same day each month as your sign-up date. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime.

### 16. Do you offer discounts for annual subscriptions?
Currently, we don't offer annual subscription discounts, but we're considering this feature for the future. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified of any pricing changes.

## Troubleshooting

### 17. I can't log in to my account. What should I do?
First, ensure you're using the correct email and password. If you're still having trouble, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset your credentials. If issues persist, contact our support team.

### 18. Why aren't my changes saving?
Check your internet connection and refresh the page. If the issue continues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. Contact support if problems persist.

### 19. My notifications aren't working properly. How do I fix this?
Go to "Settings" > "Notifications" to review and adjust your notification preferences. Ensure your email settings are correct and that our emails aren't being filtered as spam.

### 20. The Gantt chart is not displaying correctly. What can I do?
Try refreshing the page or adjusting your browser zoom level. If you're using an older browser, try updating to the latest version. Our platform works best with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### 21. I accidentally deleted an important task. Can it be recovered?
Deleted tasks are moved to the Recycle Bin where they remain for 30 days. Go to "Settings" > "Recycle Bin" to restore deleted items.

### 22. My team member isn't receiving notifications. How do I fix this?
Ask your team member to check their notification settings in "Profile" > "Preferences". Also verify that their email address is correct in the team member list.

## Integrations

### 23. How do I connect my Google Calendar?
Go to "Settings" > "Integrations" > "Google Calendar" and click "Connect Account". You'll be redirected to Google to authorize the connection.

### 24. Can I export data to Excel or CSV?
Yes, most reports can be exported to Excel or CSV format. Look for the export button in the top-right corner of any report view.

### 25. Does the mobile app have all the features of the web version?
Our mobile app supports most core features including task management, notifications, and file access. However, some advanced features like Gantt charts and detailed reporting are only available on the web version.
